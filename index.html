↑ Introducing Zoho for Healthcare — Marketing Asset

01
Market & Customer Readiness
  • Defined ICP by industry, clinic size, and decision-maker persona — targeting clinics and hospital networks
  • Identified primary use cases and pain points — administrative burden, patient scheduling, compliance gaps
  • Mapped competitive landscape: Zoho suite differentiation, compliance advantages, and pricing positioning vs. incumbents
02
Positioning & Messaging
  • Crafted positioning statement — Who / What / Why us — anchored to healthcare-specific outcomes
  • Defined value propositions across functional (workflow efficiency) and emotional (peace of mind, trust) dimensions
  • Built messaging hierarchy: platform → feature → persona, with proof points from customer stories and benchmarks
  • Aligned narrative with product vision to ensure message-market fit over time
03
Packaging & Pricing
  • Defined tiered plans with feature comparison against key competitors in the healthcare SaaS space
  • Aligned pricing with value metrics — usage-based, seat-based, and outcome-linked considerations
  • Validated strategy with sales team to ensure field alignment and objection readiness before launch
04
Product & Onboarding Readiness
  • Defined onboarding flow end-to-end: sales query → presales demo → implementation → nurture email sequences
  • Partnered with Presales and CS to ensure smooth handoff and consistent messaging across all touchpoints
05
Sales Enablement
  • Built pitch deck: problem → solution → ROI narrative structured for C-suite healthcare decision-makers
  • Created battlecards with competitor feature comparison sheets and objection-handling frameworks
  • Trained sales and presales teams on positioning, key differentiators, and common objections
06
Marketing Assets
  • Built SEO + conversion-optimised landing page targeting high-intent healthcare search queries
  • Produced product explainer video and customer testimonial video (Dr. Spine case study)
  • Published launch blog and thought leadership articles on Zoho Healthcare Digest
  • Designed pre-launch, launch, and nurture email sequences across the buyer journey
  • Created social and LinkedIn community content — including CEO newsletter for hospital stakeholders
07
Demand Generation Plan
  • Defined channel mix: organic content, paid search/social, LinkedIn ABM, partnerships
  • Built keyword and SEO strategy targeting high-intent healthcare practice management queries
  • Structured paid campaigns: search, social, and retargeting across funnel stages
08
Metrics & Success Criteria
  • North star metric: qualified lead generation for the healthcare vertical
  • Funnel metrics tracked: CTR → CAC → MQL → SQL → Conversion
  • Win rate and deal velocity tracked per segment (clinic vs. hospital network)
09
Post-Launch Optimisation
